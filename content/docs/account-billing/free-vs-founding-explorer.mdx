**Free** is meant to be genuinely useful: enough room to plan real trips and explore the map without feeling baited into paying on day two. It includes a **small monthly quota** for **[AI booking import](/trips/importing-bookings)** so you can try the workflow.

**Founding Explorer** is a **one-time** upgrade: much higher caps on trips, places per trip, wishlist, collaboration, and **import**, plus map themes, community, invites, and a **lifetime discount** on future Navi / AI add-ons when those bill separately.

**Explorer (subscription)** — when offered in-app — is for travelers who want **unlimited-style** core caps and top import / Navi quotas. It does not replace Founding perks; Founding members can add Explorer later if they want more headroom.

Exact numbers and phase (beta vs public) live in the product: open **[Pricing](https://app.nomu.land/pricing)** on [app.nomu.land](https://app.nomu.land).

## How to choose

- Start free until you bump a limit. Nomu will tell you what you hit—trips, places, wishlist pins, or something else.
- Upgrade when the limit starts shaping your behavior (“I guess I will delete that trip”) instead of your actual plans.
