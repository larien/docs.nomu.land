**Free** is meant to be genuinely useful: enough room to plan real trips and explore the map without feeling baited into paying on day two.

**Founding Explorer** is for people who live in Nomu—more trips, more places, more wishlist room, **[import from booking confirmations](/trips/importing-bookings)**, and the warm fuzzy of supporting the product while it grows. Exact numbers live in the app because they can change as we learn; open **[Pricing](https://app.nomu.land/pricing)** on [app.nomu.land](https://app.nomu.land) for the current caps and benefits.

## How to choose

- Start free until you bump a limit. Nomu will tell you what you hit—trips, places, wishlist pins, or something else.
- Upgrade when the limit starts shaping your behavior (“I guess I will delete that trip”) instead of your actual plans.
