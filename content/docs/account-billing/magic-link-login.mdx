updated: 2026-04-04
---

Magic links mean you do not maintain a Nomu-specific password. You enter your email, Nomu sends a short-lived link, and that link proves it is you.

If anything goes wrong with delivery or timing, start with [I didn't receive the magic link](/troubleshooting/magic-link-not-received).
