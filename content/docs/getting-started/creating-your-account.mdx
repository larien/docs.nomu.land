Getting started is simple: ask Nomu to email you a **magic link**. Open that message on the **same device** where you want to travel with Nomu, tap the link, and you are in—no extra password to memorize or lose behind the couch.

If the email plays hide-and-seek, do not panic. [I didn't receive the magic link](/troubleshooting/magic-link-not-received) walks through the usual fixes (spam folders, typos, grumpy corporate filters).
