Want Nomu to feel like **your** app? Install it as a **PWA** (Progressive Web App): you get a proper icon, a full-screen window, and less “which tab was that again?” energy. Same account as the browser—just a smoother front door.

Head to **[app.nomu.land](https://app.nomu.land)** for the steps below (that is the live app; the marketing site on nomu.land is separate).

## Android (Chrome)

1. Open [app.nomu.land](https://app.nomu.land) in Chrome.
2. Tap the menu (three dots) → **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.
3. Confirm. Launch Nomu from the new icon.

## iOS (Safari)

1. Open [app.nomu.land](https://app.nomu.land) in Safari.
2. Tap **Share**, then **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Name it and tap **Add**. Open Nomu from your home screen.

## Desktop (Chrome, Edge, Arc, Brave)

1. Open [app.nomu.land](https://app.nomu.land).
2. Look for **Install** in the address bar or the browser menu.
3. Accept the install. Nomu opens in its own window.

## Tips

- You stay on the same account as in the browser; there is no separate “app login.”
- After install, [sharing a map link or place into Nomu](/getting-started/share-into-nomu) from another app is often smoother on mobile.
