New here? Nomu might nudge you through a short **onboarding** tour: a warm welcome, finding a first place, spinning up a trip, and a tiny **“you did it”** moment when the map lights up. Love a guided path? Follow every step. Already know the drill? Skip what you do not need.

This is not gatekeeping—it is momentum. Once you have **one trip** and **one place**, the rest of the app has something real to show you, and everything clicks faster.
