Found a place in another app and do not want to lose it? On mobile, your system **Share** sheet can pass a title, snippet, or URL straight into Nomu (installed PWA or browser—both work). Nomu figures out what you shared and helps you put it in the right trip.

## How to use it

1. Open the map, article, or chat with the spot you are excited about.
2. Hit **Share**, then choose **Nomu** (or your browser shortcut if you have not installed the PWA yet).
3. Wrap it up inside Nomu—pick or create a trip, confirm the place, done.

## Tips

- Clean, specific shares (a maps link, a named venue) work best; huge blocks of text are harder to interpret.
- If the next screen is not what you expected, try sharing something tighter or start the trip yourself and add the place from there.
