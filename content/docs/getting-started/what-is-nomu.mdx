**Nomu** is home for the trips you actually care about. Every adventure deserves a place to live: plan it, live it, then relive it—without opening a spreadsheet or losing the story in a dozen apps.

You drop **places** on a map, tuck **notes and trip details** beside them, and **share** a trip when it feels right (beautiful public pages, on your terms). Got a “someday” café or trail? Park it on your **Wishlist** so the idea does not evaporate.

### Where you'll hang out

Three big rooms, same cozy product:

- **Trips** — timelines that make sense: dates, transport, stays, and the little details that matter.
- **Explore** — your world at a glance: pins, country highlights, and that “whoa, I have been busy” feeling.
- **Wishlist** — dreams waiting for a trip, still on the map with everything else.

It's built around **you** and **your** places—privacy-first, no attention tricks, shaped by people who travel for real. Ready? [Create your account](/getting-started/creating-your-account), then [take your first trip](/getting-started/your-first-trip) and see it click.
