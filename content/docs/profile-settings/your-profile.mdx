---
title: Your profile
description: What lives on your Nomu profile and where to change it.
updated: 2026-04-04
---

Your profile is the public-ish face of **you** inside Nomu: the name people see on invites or shared trips, and the basics tied to your account. Avatar, display name, and similar fields are edited from the profile area in the app—look for **Profile** or **Account** in navigation on your device.
