Places are the heart of a trip. Whenever Nomu asks for a location, **search**, pick the row that matches what you mean (use city and country as hints), then **set your dates**.

## Search tips

- Try the **official name**, a **neighborhood people know**, or a **larger city** if a small venue does not show up.
- On some screens you can narrow search by region if the app offers it.

If nothing matches, see [Your first trip](/getting-started/your-first-trip) for requesting a missing place on GitHub.

## Dates

Set when you arrive and leave for that stop. On multi-stop flows, adjust each stop’s range so the story matches reality.

## Thumbnails and previews

When Nomu has a strong match, you may see a thumbnail. If it feels wrong, try a different search result—the preview follows the catalog entry, not your personal label.

## Tips

- One well-chosen stop beats several fuzzy ones.
- [Share into Nomu](/getting-started/share-into-nomu) from maps or messages when you are on mobile.
