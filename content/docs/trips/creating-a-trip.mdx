A **trip** in Nomu is the bucket that holds your places, notes, and visibility settings. You are not locking dates forever—you are giving your ideas a name so they stop floating around in five different apps.

## Name it for future-you

Pick something you will recognize in a list six months later. City + purpose works well: **Lisbon sprint**, **Thanksgiving with parents**, **Japan 2027 scratchpad**.

## Add the first place early

Trips feel real once something is on the map. Add one anchor place—even a neighborhood or hotel block—so Explore and your trip view have something to orbit around.

## Visibility

You can keep a trip private or share it later. Sharing is covered in [Sharing a trip](/trips/sharing-a-trip); you do not have to decide on day one.

## Tips

- Duplicate or vague names get painful fast. If you have two “Summer” trips, add a year or destination.
- Hitting limits? See [I can't add more trips or places](/troubleshooting/trip-place-limits).
