**Import bookings** helps you turn booking confirmations into real rows on your trip—transport, stays, and things to do—so you spend less time retyping. **Navi** reads what you upload and drafts items for you to approve.

Import is available on **all tiers** with a **monthly quota** (higher on Founding Explorer; unlimited-style allowance on **Explorer** subscription when active). If you hit the limit, the app will explain what changed and point you to **[Pricing](https://app.nomu.land/pricing)**.

## Before you start

- Add **at least one place** to the trip first. Each imported item needs to attach to a stop on that trip.
- Stay **online** for upload and processing.

## How to open it

Open the trip from **[Trips](https://app.nomu.land/trips)**, then use the trip’s **menu** (next to share) and choose the **import** option for bookings or confirmations.

## What you do

1. **Upload** screenshots or PDFs of confirmations, e-tickets, or similar. You can drag files in or paste an image when the upload screen is focused away from text fields.
2. **Run the import** when you are ready—the app will read the files. Large or busy documents can take a while; if something stalls, try fewer pages or a clearer crop.
3. **Review** what came back. Open each draft, fix anything that looks off, and say which **stop** on the trip it belongs to. Skip or discard anything you do not want.
4. **Confirm** when you are happy. Nomu adds the approved items to the trip and returns you there.

If nothing useful is extracted, try a tighter screenshot that shows names, dates, and the route or property clearly.

## Tips

- One confirmation per image or short PDF often works better than a single giant export.
- After import, edit or delete rows like anything you added by hand.
- For limits and upgrades, see [Free vs Founding Explorer](/account-billing/free-vs-founding-explorer).
