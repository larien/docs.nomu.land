**Navi keeps an eye on your trips so nothing slips through.**

Navi Nudge is Nomu’s optional readiness layer: it looks for common gaps (missing dates, overnight stays without accommodation, missing transport between stops, and simple inbound/outbound reminders) and surfaces them in the trip itinerary and on trip cards — without turning into a second checklist.

## What you’ll see

- **Trip detail**: Inline notices on the relevant days, with actions like **Add stay**, **Add transport**, or **Add dates** (for visits without dates, only **Add dates** is offered — no dismiss).
- **Trip cards (home & trips list)**: A **vague** line such as “Needs attention” or “3 things need attention” when there are undismissed **high** severity gaps and the trip starts within your **card attention window** (defaults to 60 days). Cards never list individual gap titles.
- **Place rail (mobile)**: A small **count badge** on stops with undismissed high-severity gaps (decorative; details stay on the itinerary).
- **Trip overflow menu**: **Trip warnings** toggles Navi Nudge for that trip only (PATCH `gap_detection_enabled`).

## Preferences

Under **Settings → Preferences → Navi Nudge**:

- **Enable Navi Nudge** — global master switch (`gap_detection_enabled`). When off, the API returns no gaps.
- **Card attention window** — days before trip start when list cards may show the vague attention line (`gap_visibility_days`, default 60).

Dismissals are stored **per user** in preferences (`nudge_dismissals` keyed by trip id) so they survive reloads; collaborative trips keep dismissals per member.

## Silent success

When everything is resolved or dismissed, there is **no** “all clear” banner — the itinerary simply has no nudges, cards show no attention line, and rail counts disappear.

## API (for integrators)

- `GET /api/v1/trips/{id}` returns `gaps` (always a JSON array, possibly empty) and `dismissed_gap_ids` for that trip.
- Trip list responses include `high_severity_gap_count` (undismissed high-severity unique gap ids) for card summaries.
- `PATCH /api/v1/trips/{id}` accepts `gap_detection_enabled` with `version` for optimistic locking.
- `PATCH /api/v1/users/{id}/settings/preferences` accepts `gap_detection_enabled`, `gap_visibility_days`, and partial `nudge_dismissals` maps.
