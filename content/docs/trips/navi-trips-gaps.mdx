description: Trip gap detection and preferences in Nomu

**Navi keeps an eye on your trips so nothing slips through.**

**Navi: Trips Gaps** is Nomu’s optional readiness layer: it looks for common gaps (missing dates, overnight stays without accommodation, missing transport between stops, and simple inbound/outbound reminders) and surfaces them in the trip itinerary and on trip cards — without turning into a second checklist.

## What you’ll see

- **Trip detail**: Inline notices on the relevant days, with actions like **Add stay**, **Add transport**, or **Add dates** (for visits without dates, only **Add dates** is offered — no dismiss).
- **Trip cards (home & trips list)**: A **vague** “Needs attention” line (no counts) when there are undismissed **high** severity gaps and the trip starts within your **card attention window** (defaults to 60 days). It always sits on the line below the trip dates, with consistent spacing on mobile and desktop. Cards never list individual gap titles.
- **Place rail (mobile)**: A small **yellow count badge** above the corner of a stop chip when that visit has undismissed **high**-severity gaps. The number is how many distinct gaps apply to that stop (details stay in the itinerary). Tap or long-press the badge for a short explanation; the badge sits outside the photo circle so it is not clipped.
- **Trip overflow menu (⋯)**: **Navi: Trips Gaps** toggles gap hints for that trip only.

## Preferences

Under **Settings → Preferences → Navi: Trips Gaps**:

- **Enable Navi: Trips Gaps** — global master switch. When off, trips show no gap list.
- **Card attention window** — days before trip start when list cards may show the vague attention line (default 60).

Dismissals are stored **per user** in preferences, keyed by trip, so they survive reloads; collaborative trips keep dismissals per member.

## Silent success

When everything is resolved or dismissed, there is **no** “all clear” banner — the itinerary simply has no nudges, cards show no attention line, and rail counts disappear.
