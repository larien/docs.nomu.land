Nomu gives every account a generous free tier, but there are still caps—trips, places, wishlist pins, or related counts depending on what you are doing. When you hit a wall, the app should say **what** you hit, not a mystery error.

## What to do

1. **Read the message** — It usually names the limit (trips vs places vs wishlist).
2. **Archive or merge** — Delete a scratch trip you no longer need, or combine duplicate ideas.
3. **Upgrade** — If you are living in Nomu daily, **[Pricing](https://app.nomu.land/pricing)** in the app is the honest fix: Founding Explorer raises lifetime caps; Explorer subscription (when available) adds unlimited-style headroom. You also support ongoing work.

## Why limits exist

They keep abuse cheap for bad actors and keep infrastructure predictable for everyone else. Founding Explorer is the “I am all-in” lifetime tier with higher ceilings; Explorer subscription is for unlimited-style caps when you need it.
